The About (Nosotros) section tells the story of Brasa: why it was founded, how food is prepared, and what the brand stands for. It combines an image, a blockquote, four brand pillars, and three key statistics.

## HTML structure

```html index.html
<section id="nosotros" class="landing-nosotros">
  <div class="about-visual">
    <img src="img/about.JPG" alt="Nuestra cocina"
         style="object-position: center top;">
  </div>
  <div class="about-content reveal">
    <p class="sec-label">Quiénes somos</p>
    <blockquote class="about-quote">
      "Una buena hamburguesa no necesita complicarse.
       Solo necesita el mejor ingrediente."
    </blockquote>
    <p class="about-body">
      Nacimos de la convicción de que la comida bien hecha tiene que ser
      accesible. Seleccionamos nuestra carne, horneamos el pan cada mañana
      y preparamos nuestras salsas desde cero — sin atajos.
    </p>
    <!-- pillars and stats below -->
  </div>
</section>
```

## Brand pillars

The four pillars each have an SVG icon, a title, and a one-line description:

| Pillar | Description |
|---|---|
| A la brasa siempre | Cocción lenta sobre carbón. Sin trucos. |
| Ingredientes del día | Compramos local, preparamos fresco. |
| Receta propia | Salsas, aderezos y panes. Todo nuestro. |
| Calidad sin negociar | Carne de res seleccionada, siempre. |

Each pillar follows this HTML pattern:

```html index.html
<div class="pillar">
  <svg class="pillar-ico" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none">
    <!-- SVG path here -->
  </svg>
  <p class="pillar-t">A la brasa siempre</p>
  <p class="pillar-d">Cocción lenta sobre carbón. Sin trucos.</p>
</div>
```

To add a new pillar, duplicate a `.pillar` block and update the SVG, title (`pillar-t`), and description (`pillar-d`).

## Statistics

Three stats are displayed below the pillars:

```html index.html
<div class="stats">
  <div><span class="stat-n">5+</span><span class="stat-l">Años</span></div>
  <div><span class="stat-n">12</span><span class="stat-l">Recetas</span></div>
  <div><span class="stat-n">100%</span><span class="stat-l">Artesanal</span></div>
</div>
```

Update `.stat-n` for the number and `.stat-l` for the label.

## Editing the brand story

The blockquote and body copy are plain text in `index.html`. Edit them directly:

```html index.html
<blockquote class="about-quote">
  "Your updated brand quote here."
</blockquote>
<p class="about-body">
  Your updated brand story here.
</p>
```

## Changing the about image

Replace `img/about.JPG` with your own image. The `object-position: center top` inline style controls which part of the image is visible when cropped — adjust it if your focal point is different:

```html index.html
<img src="img/about.JPG" alt="Nuestra cocina"
     style="object-position: center top;">
```

<Tip>
Use `object-position: center center` for most portrait-oriented photos, or `object-position: center 20%` to show slightly below the top.
</Tip>

## Decorative circles

The two `.s-circle` elements (`.s1` and `.s2`) are purely decorative overlays on the image with SVG flame and leaf icons. They're styled in `landing.css` and can be hidden or repositioned without affecting functionality.
